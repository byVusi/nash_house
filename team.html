Meet the Team

From the Housemaster

Welcome to Nash House, a place where tradition, camaraderie, and excellence unite to create an environment of growth and support.

In Nash House, we pride ourselves on being more than just a house - we are a family of just over 100 boys, both boarders and day-boys, who share a common commitment to one another's success. As Housemaster, it is my privilege to oversee your journey, ensuring you are cared for, guided, and inspired to reach your full potential in every aspect of College life.

The house system is the heart of St John's College, and within Nash House, each boy is truly known and valued. Together with your tutor, I will strive to understand your unique personality, talents, and aspirations so that we can support you in your academic, cultural, spiritual, and sporting endeavours.

Leadership plays a key role in our house. The Head of House and our team of House Prefects work tirelessly to organise events and model the highest standards of behaviour and integrity. They are here to help you settle in, lead by example, and ensure every Nash boy feels empowered to contribute meaningfully to our community.

Your time in Nash House will be filled with opportunities to challenge yourself, whether in academics, sports, or cultural pursuits. Balancing these commitments is a skill that takes time to develop, and I encourage you to take advantage of the support offered by your tutor, your peers, and our dedicated team of leaders.
