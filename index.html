Who We Are

Our Storied History

Since 1907, Nash House has been a cornerstone of St John's College. As one of its oldest houses, we take pride in our heritage of excellence.

Our Brotherhood

Home to just over 100 boys, Nash House is a diverse and supportive family. We forge lifelong bonds, celebrate individuality, and strive for greatness in every aspect of College life.

Striving to Conquer

Guided by tradition and driven by ambition, we nurture resilience, leadership, and teamwork. Nash House is a place where every boy can achieve his full potential.
